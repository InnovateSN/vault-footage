Privacy Policy

VaultFootage collects only what is necessary to provide your subscription and keep the platform secure.

Information we collect

When you create an account or complete a purchase through Gumroad, we receive basic billing details (name, email, billing country) from our payment partner. We do not store full payment information on VaultFootage servers.

If you subscribe to updates, we store your email address solely to deliver product announcements and onboarding material.

Cookies & analytics

We use privacy-friendly analytics to understand site performance. Cookies are limited to session management and analytics events that help us improve the experience. You can disable non-essential cookies at any time via your browser settings.

No advertising trackers or third-party ad pixels are embedded on VaultFootage. Gumroad may set its own cookies to process payments securely.

Your rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data by emailing innovatesolutionsnow@gmail.com.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold or ask us to stop sending marketing emails.
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by adjusting your browser or using built-in privacy controls.

We respond to all privacy requests within 30 days.

Data retention & security

Account and billing information is retained as long as you have an active subscription or as required for accounting obligations. Footage usage data is aggregated and does not include identifiable personal information.

We implement encryption in transit (HTTPS) and restrict access to operational tools to essential team members only.

Questions about this policy? Reach us at innovatesolutionsnow@gmail.com.
